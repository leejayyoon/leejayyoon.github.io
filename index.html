Research Interests

The goal of my research is injecting knowledge/constraints into neural models, primarily for natural language processing (NLP) tasks. I am broadly interested in structured prediction, multi-task learning, logical reasoning, and better representation learning for the aforementioned topics. Currently I am exploring these topics at Graduate School of Data Science (GSDS), Seoul National University (SNU), with SKI-ML lab members. Prior to joining SNU I was a postdoc associate at IESL with Professor Andrew McCallum and during my Ph.D. with my advisor Jaime Carbonell.
